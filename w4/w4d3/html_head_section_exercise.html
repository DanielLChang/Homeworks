Ingredients

  • 1 to 3 teaspoons flavor base — soup stock paste, miso paste, curry paste
  • 1 to 3 teaspoons flavoring extras, optional — chili-garlic sauce, soy sauce or tamari
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup filler ingredients — greens, leftover cooked meat, egg
  • 3/4 to 1 cup noodles — ramen, udon, vermicelli
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup fresh ingredients — fresh herbs, sliced green onions, bean sprouts, sliced lime

Instant Noodle Cups

Description

Every college kid's go-to meal.

Directions

  1. Place flavor base and flavor extras in the bottom of the cup
  2. Top with filler ingredients
  3. Add the noodles
  4. Finish with the fresh ingredients
  5. Seal and refrigerate
